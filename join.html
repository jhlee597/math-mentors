Join the Math Mentors Club

Create clear math resources, review with friends, and (optionally) mentor younger students.

What you’ll do

  • Write short problem sets & mini-guides in LaTeX (we’ll teach you).
  • Peer-review solutions for clarity and correctness.
  • Help with layout/design or illustrations (optional).
  • Mentor during support hours (optional).

How to join

  • Come to a meeting and pick a topic.
  • Join a small team and draft your first 1–2 page PDF.
  • Get feedback, revise, and publish to the site.
  • Interested in mentoring? We’ll train you first.

Time & commitment

1 hour/week meeting + flexible time to write or review. Tutoring is optional; writing only is welcome.

Who should join?

Anyone who likes explaining ideas clearly. You don’t need to be a contest star—just curious and reliable.

Ready to start?

Bring a friend, pick a topic, and we’ll help you publish your first resource.
