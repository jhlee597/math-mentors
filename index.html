About the club

We write concise problem sets, mini guides, and challenge packets (with full solutions), then share them with our middle school and online learners. Members can write items, edit solutions, design layouts in LaTeX, and optionally tutor.

  • Short, readable PDFs with worked examples
  • Peer review for clarity and correctness
  • Optional mentoring—on campus or virtually

How it works

1 · Plan

Pick a topic (fractions, factoring, probability…). Outline examples and problems.

2 · Draft

Write in LaTeX, keep it short, include checks and common mistakes.

3 · Review

Peer-edit for clarity and correctness; test with a small group.

4 · Publish

Add to the Resources page; share with classes and clubs.

5 · Mentor

Offer optional help sessions; collect feedback and iterate.
